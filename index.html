GET YOUR AKAN NAME BY A SIMPLE CLICK OF A BUTTON!

This application will input your birthday and gender in a form.
It will then calculate the day of the week you were born then,
Depending on your gender, it will output your Akan Name upon a simple click of a button.

ABOUT AKAN NAMES

Akan names are derived from Ghanian culture. Frequently in Ghana, children are given their first name as a 'day name which corresponds to the day of the week they were born.
